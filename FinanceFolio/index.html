Benefits of investments :

Wealth Growth: One of the primary benefits of investing is the potential for your money to grow over time. By earning returns on your investments, you can accumulate more wealth than if you simply keep your money in a savings account.
Compound Interest: Investing allows you to benefit from compound interest, which means you earn interest not only on your initial investment but also on the interest you've already earned. Over time, this can significantly boost your returns.
Tax Advantages: Certain investment accounts, like retirement accounts or tax-efficient funds, offer tax advantages that can reduce your overall tax liability.
